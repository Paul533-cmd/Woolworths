About Woolworths

History

Woolworths is a retail company in South Africa, specifically originated in Cape Town it started in the year 1931 in the month of October. What the company specialise in are things like food, clothing and homeware products. Woolworths is a company that mostly and almost always focuses on providing high quality products and is well known for its commitment to sustainability and convenient ways of selling to their customers.

Woolworths focuses on high quality Products and Sustainability.
